About

Today, companion animals are no longer extensions of a family, but a very heartfelt part of it. The city of Mumbai and its small companion animals have long awaited a one-stop healthcare facility where no stone is left unturned to treat your valued family member. At the Tata Trusts, we have recognised this plea. Small Animal Hospital Mumbai (SAHM) is India's first speciality hospital founded on the values of care, cure and comfort, for both the patients and their guardians. With the best-in-class service matched to international standards, we are here for your pets 24*7*365. Rest assured, our 98,000 square-feet facility housing every possible diagnostic and treatment specialty will offer the best advanced veterinary care as soon as you walk through the doors of the Small Animal Hospital, Mumbai.
